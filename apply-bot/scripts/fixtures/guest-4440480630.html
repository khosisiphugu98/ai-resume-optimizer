IT Systems Administrator

Industry: Manufacturing

Location: Johannesburg CBD

 

About the Role

Our client in the manufacturing industry is seeking an experienced and versatile IT Administrator to join their small, close-knit IT team.

This is an excellent opportunity for an IT professional who enjoys being an all-rounder and is comfortable supporting users across all levels of an organisation. The successful candidate will provide hands-on IT administration, user support, troubleshooting, networking and infrastructure assistance, while working closely with the IT Manager and broader business.

The ideal candidate will be reliable, adaptable and willing to take ownership in a small team environment.

 

Key Responsibilities

  • Provide general IT administration and user support across the organisation.
  • Assist with troubleshooting and resolving day-to-day IT issues.
  • Provide networking and infrastructure support.
  • Assist with system updates, maintenance and general IT administration.
  • Provide support and assistance with SYSPRO.
  • Support users with varying levels of technical knowledge, from factory-floor employees to senior management and EXCO-level stakeholders.
  • Explain technical processes clearly to non-technical users.
  • Communicate professionally and effectively across all levels of the organisation.
  • Assist with the movement and handling of IT equipment when required.
  • Take ownership of tasks and provide additional support when required.
  • Work effectively as part of a small IT team.

 

Minimum Requirements

  • Proven experience in an IT Administrator or similar IT support role.
  • Strong SYSPRO experience is essential.
  • Experience with networking and infrastructure support.
  • Strong IT administration experience.
  • Good troubleshooting and problem-solving abilities.
  • Experience with system updates and maintenance.
  • Strong general IT user support experience.
  • Excellent communication skills, with the ability to engage with both technical and non-technical users.
  • Ability to work effectively within a small IT team.

 

Ideal Candidate Profile

  • An experienced IT all-rounder.
  • Energetic, adaptable and willing to get involved where needed.
  • Reliable and able to take ownership of responsibilities.
  • Comfortable working in a hands-on environment.
  • Able to occasionally move and handle heavier IT equipment.
  • Flexible with working hours when required.
  • Comfortable supporting users at all levels, from factory employees to directors and EXCO.
  • A strong communicator who can adapt their communication style to suit different audiences.

 

Working Conditions

  • The role may occasionally require overtime and after-hours support.
  • Overtime is compensated with time off in lieu.
  • Flexible working arrangements are available, including flexi-time.
  • Remote working may be available when required, provided the successful candidate remains available and on standby
